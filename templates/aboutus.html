About Us

INDICAN Friendship Club is a virtual platform for the Indians and Canadians to connect and build healthy relationships. It is a membership based club, wherein you can subscribe on our website, engage in our weekly membership activities to keep the fun alive and also avail the services from our onboard associates in both the countries. Keeping the connection between these two countries in mind, this club allows its members to interact, celebrate, and trade virtually.

  • From making new friends and networking with them, this virtual platform will boost your ties.
  • Exciting and enjoying events will exchange our culture, thoughts and lifestyle. Hence, knowing each other better.
  • This platform will give you a one stop shop for all relevant services which you may require while travelling across India and Canada.

It is a membership based club, you can subscribe and avail our services.
This will not only strengthen the India-Canada relationship, but it will also provide Indians and Canadians with a unique opportunity to explore the two countries. Click here to KNOW MORE.

OUR AIM

Giving an extraordinary flavor to this virtual club, we believe in providing one-of-a-kind opportunities to explore both countries and its people. We also intend to do cultural exchange between the people of both the countries which will reflect both the cultures and bring people together.

OUR VISION

We aspire to connect and bring together more than 10,000 people from India and Canada through INDICAN Friendship Club and create one stop shop for them where the citizens can look for every service that they may require. This will act as a platform to network, exchange business and also build connections across the countries.
